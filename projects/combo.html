Combo

A digital channel for a hostel, music venue, art gallery, radio; all combined

In 2019, a brand new space known as Combo came to life, seamlessly blending a captivating hostel experience with a cutting-edge contemporary art gallery and a vibrant radio station. This dynamic space offers a vibrant experience for people to revel in joyous moments of relaxation and entertainment. With the idea to expand its reach to three prominent Italian cities Milan, Venice, Bologna, Combo sought the expertise of Leftloft to forge a fresh and captivating webstie. We worked on an exciting journey to create a website that not only attracts residents with Combo but also showcases its distinctive blend of hospitality and cultural enrichment. The result is a digital platform that includes the essence of both the welcoming spirit of hospitality and the vibrant world of Italian emergent musicians and artists.
