About Huiling Li

I am a designer based in Amsterdam and Milan, specializing in visual and strategic work that resonate with contemporary culture. Through close collaboration with individuals and companies, I craft branding systems, products, and experiences that aim to catch the zeitgeist. Whether it's large-scale projects reaching significant audiences or socially and culturally motivated initiatives, I try to make a difference through my work.

Throughout my career, I have had the opportunity to work on a diverse range of projects. From collaborating with global marketing firms and digital product companies to working with independent artists and cultural institutions, I have gained valuable experience across various industries. My works includes creating brands, developing products, designing books, crafting websites, launching campaigns, and curating immersive experiences.
