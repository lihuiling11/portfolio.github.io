Italics is the first digital editorial platform that promotes the culture and beauty of Italy through the voices of Italian art gallerists, including international leading galleries Gagosian, Galleria Continua, Massimo De Carlo. Working together and sharing experiences, on and offline, with an international audience of collectors and art lovers, Italics spotlights Italy and what makes it extraordinary by sharing gallerists’ very personal experience and tips. With Leftloft, we have designed and developed visual identity, naming, a website, and the IG social channel @italics.art of Italics.

Galleria Raffaella Cortese is a contemporary art gallery based in Milan since 1995 representing a transgenerational group of 30 global artists, including Roni Horn, Kiki Smith, Kimsooja. On the occasion of its’ twenty-fifth anniversary, with Leftloft we designed a brand new identity, the website and the social media communication formats. The choice of font gc16 typeface by foundry Bold decisions a monospaced yet organic typeface, combining digital and humanistic traits, emphasising exactly the characters of the gallery.

Land Convention 2019

Campaign and Spatial Design

Campaign and
Environmental design

close

LAND is an international landscape architecture studio with offices in Italy, Switzerland, and Germany. In the year 2019 LAND had held the first convention in Milan, for their colleagues from 3 European offices to discuss the future of architecture and urban landscape. With Leftloft we worked on the campaign and spatial design of the convention. Specially we designed 3 foldable brochures, which present 3 offices of LAND. On the front side, we have collected all the case studies of 3 offices, at the backside, 3 brochures are 3 puzzles that can be connected and eventually become a whole landscape of 30 years of LAND.
