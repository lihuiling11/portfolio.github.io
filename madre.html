Back in 2013, Leftloft designed a whole new visual identity for Madre museum, a contemporary art museum located in Napoli. In 2018, Madre has asked for a review of its identity, to satisfy the needs of a more complex institutional environment. We have redesigned the corporate image and renewed the website. The new identity has been launched with a campaign for an exhibition about legendary american photographer Robert Mapplethorpe.
